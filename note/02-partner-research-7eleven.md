## Key Insights for Pitch

### Value Propositions for CP ALL

1. **Drive Foot Traffic**
   - Users who know a branch has their item will visit
   - Reduces "no purchase" store visits

2. **Clear Inventory**
   - Help branches move slow-selling items
   - Display availability encourages purchases

3. **New Product Launches**
   - Promote new products to interested customers
   - Track which branches have launched which products

4. **Customer Insights**
   - Data on what customers are searching for
   - Demand signals by location

5. **Reduced Customer Frustration**
   - Better customer experience
   - Less wasted trips = happier customers

## Detailed Competitor Analysis

### 1. Grab (GrabMart)

**Overview:**
- Multi-service platform (ride-hailing, food, delivery, payments)
- GrabMart for grocery and convenience store items
- Strong presence in Thailand

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Massive user base and brand recognition
- ✅ Integrated delivery infrastructure
- ✅ Multiple store options (7-Eleven, Tops, Big C)
- ✅ Fast delivery (30 mins - 1 hour)

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ No inventory visibility across branches
- ❌ Users can't check which physical store has items
- ❌ Delivery fees can be expensive
- ❌ Not optimized for "go get it yourself" use case

**Our Differentiation:**
> Grab helps you get things delivered. We help you know WHERE to go if you want to pick up yourself (faster, free).

---

### 2. LINE MAN

**Overview:**
- Delivery platform popular in Thailand
- Food, grocery, and messenger services
- Integration with LINE app ecosystem

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Strong Thai market presence
- ✅ LINE ecosystem integration (chat, pay)
- ✅ Distance and delivery time shown
- ✅ Local restaurant and store partnerships

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ No branch-level inventory search
- ❌ Focused on delivery, not pickup
- ❌ Limited to their partner stores

**Our Differentiation:**
> LINE MAN shows you distance to delivery riders. We show you distance to stores WITH your item in stock.

---

### 3. Shopee

**Overview:**
- E-commerce marketplace (Alibaba-backed Sea Group)
- Strong in Southeast Asia including Thailand
- Mall-style multi-seller platform

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Huge product selection
- ✅ Aggressive discounts and vouchers
- ✅ Visual search capability exists
- ✅ ShopeePay integration

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ Online only - no physical store integration
- ❌ 1-7 day shipping times
- ❌ No real-time convenience store inventory
- ❌ Not for immediate needs

**Our Differentiation:**
> Shopee is for planned purchases that can wait. We're for "I want this NOW" at a store near me.

---

### 4. Lazada

**Overview:**
- E-commerce platform (Alibaba Group)
- Wide product selection
- LazMall for official brand stores

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Large product catalog
- ✅ Official brand stores (LazMall)
- ✅ Coupons and sales events
- ✅ Visual search feature

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ Online only - no physical store connection
- ❌ Shipping takes 1-7 days
- ❌ Not for immediate purchases

**Our Differentiation:**
> Same as Shopee - we complement e-commerce for instant gratification needs.

---

### 5. 7-Eleven App (7 App / ALL ONLINE)

**Overview:**
- Official CP ALL mobile app
- Loyalty program, delivery, and online shopping
- Direct integration with 7-Eleven ecosystem

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Official app with accurate product info
- ✅ ALL Member loyalty points
- ✅ 7Delivery for delivery
- ✅ ALL ONLINE for extended catalog
- ✅ Store locator built-in

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ NO branch-level inventory visibility
- ❌ Can't search "which store near me has X"
- ❌ Store locator shows location, not stock
- ❌ ALL ONLINE ≠ in-store availability

**Our Differentiation:**
> The 7-Eleven app tells you where stores ARE. We tell you where your PRODUCT is.

## Key Differentiators Summary

| What We Do | What Others Don't |
|------------|-------------------|
| Show real-time inventory by branch | Others show products, not locations |
| Help users visit stores (pickup) | Others focus on delivery |
| Image-based product search for physical stores | Image search exists but for online only |
| Multi-chain search (future) | Platforms are single-chain or marketplace |
| Free to find, pay only for extended radius | Competitors charge for delivery |

---

## Threats & Responses

| Threat | Response Strategy |
|--------|-------------------|
| 7-Eleven builds this feature internally | Move fast, build user base, become acquisition target |
| Grab adds inventory search | Partner with multiple chains, not just one |
| Users prefer delivery over pickup | Offer optional delivery through partners |
| Low user adoption | Focus on high-demand products (new releases, limited items) |

---

## Opportunities

1. **Underserved Niche**: No one solves "where can I physically buy X right now?"
2. **Partnership Value**: We drive foot traffic, which stores want
3. **Data Insights**: We learn what people are searching for (valuable to retailers)
4. **Expansion**: Start with convenience stores, expand to pharmacies, electronics, etc.
